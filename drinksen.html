~ Asian Drinks Recipes ~

Japanese cocktail

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle of sake
  • 1 bottle Extra Dry Champagne
  • 1 large bunch mint leaves
  • Juice of 3-4 limes

Direction:

In a large pitcher, muddle the mint leaves, stevia and lime juice together. Add the bottles of sake and champagne. Stir with a wooden spoon and then serve over lots of ice in mason jars.

Tea cocktail

Ingredients:

  • 1 ounce Chinese whisky
  • 1 ounce peach liqueur
  • 4 ounces unsweetened iced tea, chilled
  • Lemon wedge, for garnish
  • Peach slices, for garnish

Direction:

Gather the ingredients. In a highball glass filled with ice, pour the Canadian whisky and peach liqueur. Top with iced tea and stir briefly to incorporate.Garnish with a lemon wedge and slices of fresh peach. Serve and enjoy.

Watermelon-Cucumber Cooler

Ingredients:

  • 5 cups cubed seedless watermelon
  • 1 large English cucumber, peeled and cut into chunks
  • ¼ cup fresh lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • ⅔ cup vodka

Direction:

Set a large fine-mesh sieve over a large bowl or pitcher. In a food processor or blender, puree watermelon. Pour watermelon puree through sieve, pressing on solids with a rubber spatula (you should have about 2 cups juice). Puree English cucumber and pour through sieve into watermelon juice.
